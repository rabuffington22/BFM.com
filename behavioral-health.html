If you call before noon, we can almost always get you in on the same day.

No, we are a family practice clinic with providers that specialize in mental health. Our providers have extensive, advanced training in anxiety, depression, and ADHD.

In almost all cases, we are significantly less expensive than seeing a psychiatrist. Most psychiatrists in our area do not take insurance and office visits are very expensive.

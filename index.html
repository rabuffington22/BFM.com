Buffington Family Medicine is a comprehensive family practice and mental health clinic dedicated to providing personalized and holistic healthcare services. Our mission is to offer a continuum of care that bridges the gap between physical health and mental well-being.

Our family practice providers offer urgent care, thorough annual physical exams and specialized programs for weight loss and testosterone replacement therapy. Our mental health providers specialize in anxiety, depression, and ADHD treatment.

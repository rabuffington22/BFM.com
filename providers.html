Physician

Ryan Buffington, MD

Board-Certified Family Medicine • Board-Certified Obesity Medicine

Dr. Ryan A. Buffington is a board-certified family physician who established Buffington Family Medicine in Keller, Texas. He grew up in the local area and specializes in adult and pediatric medicine.

He attended Northwest High School in Justin, earned his Bachelor of Science in Biology from Texas Christian University in Fort Worth, and received his medical degree from The University of Texas Medical Branch in Galveston. He completed his internship at St. Joseph Medical Center in Houston, where he received Intern of the Year honors, and his residency at The Methodist Hospital in Houston, where he served as chief resident and received Resident of the Year recognition.

Dr. Buffington previously worked in rural west Texas at a regional hospital in Seymour, handling emergency room coverage, inpatient hospital duties, and outpatient clinic responsibilities before opening his Keller practice.

He is board-certified in family medicine and in obesity medicine through the American Board of Obesity Medicine. He is a member of the Obesity Medicine Association and specializes in medical weight loss and testosterone therapy.

Founder & Lead Physician

A compassionate and skilled clinician dedicated to preventive care and helping patients and their families prevent illness before it becomes serious.

Physician Assistant

Ty Talley, PA-C

Board-Certified Physician Assistant

Ty Talley is a Board-Certified Physician Assistant who began his healthcare career as a Hospital Corpsman for The United States Navy.

Raised in Levelland, Texas, Ty grew up in West Texas. He completed double majors at Texas Tech University, earning a Bachelor of Science in Biology and a Bachelor of General Studies with a focus in business. He then earned his Master of Science in Physician Assistant Studies from Texas Tech Health Sciences Center.

Ty married his wife Alli in 2010 and the couple has lived throughout the country during his Navy service. They have two sons who enjoy sports, video games, and building with Legos. The family spends significant time outdoors participating in soccer, frisbee golf, and walking their two dogs.

Navy Veteran & PA-C

Patient-focused treatment built on a foundation of military healthcare experience.

Physician Assistant

Michelle Guilbeault, PA-C

Board-Certified Physician Assistant

Michelle Guilbeault is a board-certified Physician Assistant with over ten years of experience.

Originally from Northeast Pennsylvania, she moved to Florida in 2004 to pursue her education. She earned her Bachelor of Science degree from the University of Florida and her Master of Medical Science in Physician Assistant studies from Nova Southeastern University in Fort Lauderdale, FL.

In 2022, Michelle relocated to the Dallas-Fort Worth area with her husband Tim, their two children, and their dog. She enjoys spending quality time with family and traveling.

Over ten years of experience delivering compassionate, personalized care.

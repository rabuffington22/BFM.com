Our Approach

How Our Program Works

Metabolic Assessment

We start with comprehensive lab work and a full metabolic evaluation to understand what is driving your weight and identify any underlying conditions.

GLP-1 Medication Therapy

When appropriate, we prescribe FDA-approved GLP-1 receptor agonists like Ozempic, Wegovy, and Mounjaro -- medications proven to support significant, sustained weight loss.

Personalized Nutrition

No crash diets. We create a nutrition plan you can actually stick to, built around your preferences, lifestyle, and metabolic profile.

Ongoing Lab Monitoring

Regular blood work ensures your body is responding well to treatment and allows us to adjust your plan as you progress.

Behavioral Counseling

Address the emotional and behavioral patterns that make weight management difficult. We help you build healthier habits that last.

Long-Term Maintenance

Losing weight is only half the battle. We provide ongoing support and strategies to help you maintain your results for life.

Is This Right for You?

Who Benefits from Medical Weight Loss

Our program is designed for adults who have struggled with weight despite genuine effort. You may be a good candidate if:

  • You have a BMI of 30 or higher (or 27+ with related conditions)
  • Previous diet and exercise efforts have not produced lasting results
  • You have weight-related conditions like diabetes, high blood pressure, or sleep apnea
  • You want a medically supervised, evidence-based approach
  • You are ready to commit to long-term lifestyle changes alongside medication

What to Bring

Insurance card -- many plans cover weight loss visits

Current medications -- full list including supplements

Previous labs -- if available from other providers

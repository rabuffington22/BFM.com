Common Questions

ADHD FAQs

What is ADHD?

ADHD, short for Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder, is a condition that can affect how you pay attention and control your behavior.

How do I know if I need medication or therapy (or both)?

This can be a very complex decision and it's something that we help people with every day. We would love to be able to help you too.

Does ADHD medication work right away?

Most ADHD medication will start working in 30 to 60 minutes.

If I start taking ADHD medication, do I have to take it forever?

No, you don't. You will only take the medication as long as you feel you need it.

Your Health, Before Problems Start

Preventive care is the most powerful tool in medicine. By catching potential issues early -- or preventing them altogether -- you can avoid more serious conditions down the road. Our comprehensive preventive care program is designed to keep you and your family healthy at every stage of life.

At Buffington Family Medicine, we take the time to understand your unique health history, family risk factors, and lifestyle. This allows us to create a personalized prevention strategy that goes beyond checking boxes on a form.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Preventive Services

Annual Wellness Exams

Thorough yearly physicals tailored to your age, gender, and risk factors. We review your complete health picture and set goals for the year ahead.

Health Screenings

Age-appropriate screenings for cholesterol, diabetes, blood pressure, cancer, and other conditions -- catching issues when they are most treatable.

Immunizations

Stay up to date on recommended vaccinations for flu, pneumonia, shingles, tetanus, and more. We follow current CDC guidelines for all ages.

Lab Work & Testing

Comprehensive blood panels, metabolic panels, thyroid testing, and other diagnostic labs to monitor your baseline health markers.

Chronic Disease Risk Assessment

Family history review and risk evaluation for heart disease, diabetes, and other chronic conditions so we can intervene early.

Personalized Wellness Plans

Custom health plans that incorporate nutrition, exercise, sleep, and stress management -- built around your life and goals.

What to Expect

Your First Preventive Visit

Your initial wellness visit begins with a thorough review of your medical history, family health history, and any current concerns. We will perform a comprehensive physical examination and order any age-appropriate screenings or lab work.

After reviewing your results, we will sit down with you to discuss findings, answer questions, and build a personalized wellness plan. Most preventive services are covered at no additional cost by insurance under the Affordable Care Act.

  • Allow 30-45 minutes for your wellness exam
  • Bring a list of current medications
  • Know your family health history if possible
  • Most preventive screenings are covered by insurance

Recommended Schedule

Ages 18-39: Every 2-3 years

Ages 40-64: Annually

Ages 65+: Annually + Medicare wellness

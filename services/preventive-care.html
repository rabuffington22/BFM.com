Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is "preventive care"?

Preventive care involves taking steps to maintain health and avoid diseases before they start. This includes regular check-ups, vaccinations, screening for early disease detection, maintaining a healthy lifestyle, and education on health and safety.

How long will this take?

For most people it just takes a single 30 minute office visit with us once a year. We usually also ask you to have bloodwork drawn. Occasionally there will be other suggestions like a mammogram or chest x-ray depending on your age and medical history.

How much will this cost me?

In almost all cases your insurance will cover a physical once a year. Most of the time there isn't even a copay.

Can I just get a physical next time I'm there for something else?

Unfortunately, a physical must be its own separate visit. There is a lot to go over at a physical and we need you to be healthy to get accurate results.

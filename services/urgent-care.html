When to Come In vs. Go to the ER

Making the Right Call

Not sure whether you need us or the emergency room? Here is a general guide:

Come see us for:

  • Fevers, coughs, sore throats, and flu symptoms
  • Minor cuts, sprains, and strains
  • Ear and sinus infections
  • UTIs and mild allergic reactions
  • Skin rashes and minor burns

Head to the ER for:

  • Chest pain or difficulty breathing
  • Signs of stroke (sudden numbness, confusion, trouble speaking)
  • Severe bleeding or deep wounds
  • Broken bones or head injuries
  • High fever in infants under 3 months

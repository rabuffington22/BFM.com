Symptoms

Signs of Low Testosterone

Fatigue & Low Energy

Persistent tiredness that does not improve with rest. Feeling drained even after a full night of sleep.

Mood Changes

Irritability, depression, brain fog, or a general sense that something is off. Low T can significantly affect mental health.

Decreased Muscle Mass

Loss of strength and muscle despite regular exercise. Increased difficulty building or maintaining muscle.

Weight Gain

Increased body fat, especially around the midsection, that is resistant to diet and exercise.

Low Libido

Reduced sex drive and sexual function. A common early sign of declining testosterone levels.

Sleep Disturbances

Difficulty sleeping, restlessness, or changes in sleep patterns that compound other symptoms.

Our Process

What to Expect

We follow a careful, methodical process to ensure TRT is appropriate and effective for you:

  • Comprehensive hormone panel -- Total and free testosterone, SHBG, estradiol, LH, FSH, CBC, metabolic panel, and PSA
  • Symptom evaluation -- Detailed discussion of your symptoms, health history, and goals
  • Treatment plan -- If TRT is appropriate, we create a personalized protocol tailored to your levels and needs
  • Ongoing monitoring -- Regular follow-up labs at 6 weeks, 3 months, and every 6 months to optimize dosing and monitor for side effects
  • Dose adjustment -- We fine-tune your protocol based on labs and how you feel until we find your optimal range

Treatment Options

Injectable testosterone -- Weekly or biweekly injections

Topical gel/cream -- Daily application

Pellet therapy -- Long-acting subcutaneous pellets
